What started as a recount of my trip to Japan for my sponsors turned into a blog hailing about all sorts of trips, from visiting family to just a couple of hours away in the mountains.

Here I am in my rawest form. As I do a lot of things in my spare time, it's nice to show what I've been doing and how much time I've been putting into work in the form of a blog with hasty updates and unfinished work.

As a supplement to my travel blog, I've also taken photos from my travels and from little things I see in every day life. Finding magic in mundane things makes the world more beautiful.

It's a work in progress
