What is a youtaite?

A hobby where people from around the world collaborate to make a cover of a song. It requires the collaboration of people who sing, who can mix, make the video, and make some kind of art (sometimes). This is entirely a hobby and can be aggravating frustrating as projects get abandoned or fall through due to people ghosting or to poor leadership. The age range of participants is usually from middle school to people in their late 20's.

It boils down to "weeb karaoke."

Chorus Battles

Several people will compete in chorus battles which are competitions where the same type of collaboration happens on a time crunch whilst competing against other teams.
